Commission Hype — Privacy Policy

Effective July 8, 2026

Commission Hype ("the app") is built so that your sales data belongs to you. This policy explains what information the app handles and where it goes. The short version: your activity data never reaches us — we couldn't read it if we wanted to.

Data you create in the app

Everything you log — interactions, sales, commission amounts, shifts, goals, your display name, and settings — is stored on your device and, if you're signed into iCloud, synced through your private iCloud database (Apple CloudKit). Private iCloud data is encrypted and accessible only to your Apple ID. The developer has no server and no ability to access, read, or share this data.

Subscriptions and purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store; we never see your payment details. To validate your subscription across your devices, the app uses RevenueCat, which receives your purchase receipt and a randomly generated anonymous identifier (not your name, email, or Apple ID). RevenueCat processes this solely to confirm your subscription status. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no ad networks.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of personal information.
  • No accounts — the app never asks for your email or password.

Photos

If you save a brag graphic, the app writes the image to your photo library with your permission. Nothing is uploaded.

Deleting your data

Settings → Danger Zone → "Reset all data" deletes everything the app has stored, on-device and in your private iCloud database. Deleting the app and its iCloud data from iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud accomplishes the same.

Children

The app is a professional sales tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
